BLINKIT SALES DASHBOARD – EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This dashboard presents a consolidated view of Blinkit’s sales performance across products, outlet types, outlet sizes, and locations. It is designed to help stakeholders quickly understand business trends and identify growth opportunities.

OVERALL PERFORMANCE
Total Sales: $1,000,000
Average Sales: $141
Number of Items Sold: 8,523
Average Customer Rating: 3.9

Sales show a generally stable upward trend with a peak performance in 2018, indicating strong market demand during that period.

PRODUCT INSIGHTS
Regular-fat products contribute the majority of revenue compared to low-fat items.
Top-performing item categories include:
- Fruits and Snacks
- Household items
- Frozen and Dairy products

Lower-performing categories such as Seafood, Breakfast, and Starch represent potential areas for expansion or optimization.

OUTLET PERFORMANCE
Outlet Size:
- High-size outlets generate the highest sales ($508K, ~42%)
- Medium-size outlets contribute $445K
- Small outlets contribute $249K

Outlet Location:
- Tier 3 outlets lead in total sales ($472K)
- Tier 2 outlets generate $393K
- Tier 1 outlets generate $336K

Outlet Type:
- Supermarket Type 1 is the top performer with $788K in sales and the highest item count
- Grocery Stores and other supermarket types contribute moderate but consistent sales

KEY BUSINESS TAKEAWAYS
- High-size outlets and Supermarket Type 1 locations are primary revenue drivers
- Regular-fat products dominate customer demand
- Tier 3 locations present strong sales potential and should be prioritized
- Underperforming product categories offer opportunities for targeted growth strategies

This dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by providing clear visibility into sales trends, product performance, and outlet efficiency.
